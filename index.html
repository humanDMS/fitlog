Apple Health Sync

Web apps can't read HealthKit directly. Here are your options:

Method 1 – Health App
Open Health → Summary → tap Activity → Active Energy to see today's total. Enter it manually here.

Method 2 – Apple Shortcuts
Create a Shortcut: Get Health Samples (type: Active Energy, today) → Get Numbers from Input → Copy to Clipboard. Run it before opening FitLog, then paste.

Method 3 – Apple Watch
Swipe up on watch face → Activity ring → scroll to see Active calories.
